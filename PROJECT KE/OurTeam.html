Achieng Kuir

Chief Sustainability Officer

Achieng Kuir is a compassionate UWC student who studied social entrepreneurship and ethical leadership at amala education. Privileged to get education both in a refugee camp and UWC Waterford she wants to advocate for refugee education and be part of a community that wants to make a difference. She believes that educating the community and empowering girls can be the most essential means to solve the challenges

." Our challenges can be understood backwards; but we must prepare for the forward." -Achieng Kuir.

Falastin Mohamud

Treasurer

Falastin Mohamud is a dedicated Somalian who is passionate and emphasize about girl child education and solving some of the community most challenging hurdles.She is an alumnus at UWC Dilijan in Armenia and has the desire to continue the mission of UWC of making education a force of uniting people.She believe in inclusiveness and working with youths to create change.

" Lets stand strong to solve the problems we are in for the good of our next generation."- Falastin Mohamud.

Mary Minza

Media Ecologist

Mary Minza is a Tanzanian by nationality.She believes by working together anything is possible.She is driven by her former Tanzanian president HOn. John Pombe Magufuli when he said that " Coming together is strength and with the solidarity we can bring change in Africa when we work together and see each other as Africans."She is a former Teensinai alumnus a course that brings youths together so that they can identify the community's most pressing challenges by using technology as a means to solve them.

" It all starts with you and I to end these dreadful challenges in our community."-Mary Minza.

Ring Aken

Co-founder and Exacutive Director

Ring Aken is a compassionate South Sudanese and Kenyan by nationality. He has the desire to bring change in his post-conflict country and at large by advocating the fight against tribalism and the need for peace. .He is very passionate in bringing change and he is determined in making the World a better place to live by making the SDGs come into reality.He is motivated by the saying of Maniba Manzari that everything starts from within. .He is also very supportive of STEM ( Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics ) because he believes through science human beings can understand the universe they are in.
