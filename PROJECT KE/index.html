Women Empowerment.

Project 21 Kenya empowers women by creating life changing opportunities tailored to their needs .As part of our mission we empower women by providing them resources, support, and skills they need to transform their lives—and the lives of their family through Job training, Providing life skill courses, Charities, and access to opportunities.

Globally, women have fewer opportunities for economic participation than men, less access to basic and higher education, greater health and safety risks, and less political representation. Due to this we bring women together so that they can be part of the problem solving.

Water Relief Program: Nourishing Communities Near Kakuma Refugee Camp (Water For Life ).

Access to clean and safe water is a fundamental human right, yet countless communities around the world still lack this basic necessity. "Water for Life" initiative is a beacon of hope that brings life-giving water to both refugees and the host community near Kakuma Refugee Camp. Through this project, not only are we quenching thirst but also nurturing hope and fostering a sense of unity in a region where water scarcity has been a challenge.
